Welcome To Your ASMIT

Welcome to the ever growing academic world of Asian School of Management and Technology(ASMIT)! In the past few years since ASMIT first opened its doors, we have grown to a promising name in IT education in Nepal. We offer some of the most sought after Bachelors courses like BIM and B.Sc.CSIT in affiliation to Tribhuvan University. We are very proud of the successes that our student have demonstrated in all areas of life. Our commitment at Asian School Of Management and Technology is to provide our students an intelluctually stimulating environment that will empower students to become innovative thinkers. Creative problems solvers and inspired learners prepared to thrive in the twenty first century.
